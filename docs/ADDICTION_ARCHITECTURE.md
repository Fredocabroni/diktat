# ADDICTION ARCHITECTURE — DIKTAT

**Principle:** We're building a product people open 5+ times a day and don't regret. This maps peer-reviewed behavioral design to specific Diktat mechanics.

**Goal:** Engaged retention, not compulsive misery. TikTok is addictive AND makes users feel hollow. Duolingo is addictive AND makes users feel proud. We build toward the Duolingo pole with TikTok's hook speed.

---

## 1. FIRST PRINCIPLES

Humans don't return for features. They return because the app solved a need in a way that, on reflection, felt good. The feeling is the moat.

Four meta-drivers:
1. **Competence** — "I did a thing and got better at it."
2. **Tribe** — "My people are here, and we're winning."
3. **Curiosity** — "I'll learn something that surprises me."
4. **Revenge** — "That person is wrong and I can prove it."

Diktat combines all four. Unfair psychological advantage.

---

## 2. THE HOOKED LOOP (NIR EYAL)

Trigger → Action → Variable Reward → Investment.

| Stage | Diktat mechanic |
|---|---|
| External trigger | "The Drop is live in 10 min" push; X timeline clip; friend just beat you in trivia |
| Internal trigger | Boredom, outrage at news, desire to prove a point, FOMO on Drop |
| Action (minimum effort) | Tap app → 3 swipes on feed = 15 seconds to first AP gain |
| Variable reward | Tribe ("247 agreed"), Hunt (AP gain 15–120 per battle), Self (surprise tier-up animation) |
| Investment | AP banked, streak extended, Fingerprint deepens, tribe loyalty accrues, friend connections form |

**Rule:** Variable rewards must never feel manipulative. A loss should feel fair.

---

## 3. FOGG BEHAVIOR MODEL: B = MAP

Behavior happens when Motivation × Ability × Prompt align. If any is zero, no behavior.

**Motivation:** intrinsic (tribal pride, curiosity, outrage, self-image) + extrinsic (AP, tier-ups, eventual tier 3+ USDC micropayouts).

**Ability:** custodial wallet (no crypto friction), one-tap to battle, 15-second engagement floor, mobile default mic for voice.

**Prompts:**
- Daily Drop push at 8 PM ET local
- Streak risk push at 9 PM if mission incomplete
- Opponent match push
- Tribe rallying push
- Return-from-loss push after 24h absence

---

## 4. FLOW STATE (CSIKSZENTMIHALYI)

Flow = challenge matches skill, clear goals, immediate feedback.

- Matchmaking ±200 AP (opponents near your level)
- Real-time AP visualization during battle
- Immediate feedback on every swipe
- Graduated unlocks (new categories/tribes at tier milestones)
- Clear goals at every time horizon: 15 sec (win this swipe), 5 min (this match), 1 day (Drop + streak), 1 week (tier-up), 1 month (ghost-to-real AP at tier 3), 3 years (Mythic)

**Rule:** Matches >400 AP apart are broken. Hard cap.

---

## 5. HABIT LOOP (DUHIGG)

Cue → Routine → Reward.

Daily:
- Cue: 8 PM ET Drop push. Synchronized across users. Same time every day. Pavlovian.
- Routine: tap → read 30 sec → vote or challenge → watch tribal response
- Reward: AP + tribe leaderboard movement + social proof + next debate on deck

Weekly: Tribe Sunday. Leaderboard resets Sunday 9 PM ET.

**Rule:** Never move The Drop time. Never skip a day.

---

## 6. CIALDINI'S 7 PRINCIPLES

1. **Reciprocity** — 100 starter AP free. Public thanks for fact-checkers.
2. **Commitment & Consistency** — Fingerprint (once public, you defend it). Streaks compound commitments. Tribe membership = identity signal.
3. **Social Proof** — "247 agreed with you." Live viewer counts. Public tier badges.
4. **Authority** — Tier badges = earned authority. Vanguard+ fact-checks weigh more.
5. **Liking** — Pre-battle opponent bio + Fingerprint overlap. "Changed My Mind" badges reward vulnerability.
6. **Scarcity** — Limited-time tribe events, seasonal Mythic cosmetics, Drop is single daily event.
7. **Unity** — Tribes as identity, election-night collective witnessing.

---

## 7. SKINNER / VARIABLE-RATIO

Variable-ratio = highest resistance to extinction. We use the mechanic, not the spirit.

Applied:
- AP per battle varies within predictable band
- Opinion-shift feedback varies
- Fact-check victories occasionally featured in Theater (5-10x AP)
- Tier-up animations have 4 severity levels

**Deliberately NOT:** loot boxes, gacha, random real-money rewards. Not Skinner's casino — Skinner's classroom.

---

## 8. LOSS AVERSION

Losses feel ~2x as strong as gains.

- Streak flames visible at all times
- Ghost earnings for tiers 0–2 ("$0.47 you would have earned")
- Tier floor protection below tier 7 (early users safe)
- Loss streak protection (30% reduced loss for 2 after 3 consecutive losses)

**Rule:** No loss aversion tied to real money below tier 3.

---

## 9. FOGG TINY HABITS — ANCHORS

Every new habit rides an existing one:
- After waking + checking phone → Morning Brief card
- After lunch → 3 swipes to burn streak tick
- After bed + scrolling → 10 PM battle (Drop is 8 PM, battle window prime)

Push notifications land at anchor moments, not random times.

---

## 10. SELF-DETERMINATION THEORY

Humans need autonomy + competence + relatedness.

- Autonomy: user picks tribe, skips battles, controls Fingerprint reveal
- Competence: tier reflects skill, win rate tracked, fact-check accuracy score
- Relatedness: tribe belonging, friend challenges, election-night events

**Why:** Products satisfying SDT get longer LTV + lower churn than Skinner-only designs.

---

## 11. ANTI-PATTERNS WE REJECT

- ❌ Infinite outrage feed with no exit ramp
- ❌ Auto-playing inflammatory content
- ❌ Hidden AP costs or confusing economies
- ❌ Dopamine loops tied to real money below tier 3
- ❌ Notification spam with no user value
- ❌ "Unsubscribe is 6 clicks" patterns
- ❌ Confirm-you-really-want-to-leave modals
- ❌ Read receipts, last-seen indicators
- ❌ Artificial scarcity on non-scarce things
- ❌ Shadow bans without notification

**Guardrail:** If someone says "Facebook does this" — check if it works because it works, or works against users.

---

## 12. "DO YOU TRUST US?" TEST

Every mechanic answers: does this make users trust us more or less?

- Streaks → trust up
- Ghost earnings → trust up (transparent)
- Surprise tier-ups → trust up
- Sharing Fingerprint without asking → trust down. Never.
- 11 PM re-engagement pushes → trust down. Never.
- "You've been scrolling 30 min, take a break?" → trust UP. Build this.

## 14. RITUALS

Rituals > features. Features fade. Rituals compound.

**Daily:**
- 8 PM ET The Drop — most important ritual
- Morning Brief 6–10 AM (3 overnight topics)
- Streak lock midnight local

**Weekly:**
- Sunday 9 PM tribe reset + leaderboard close
- Friday 5 PM Week in Review

**Monthly:**
- 1st of month tier reconciliation

**Seasonal:**
- Election cycles = live mode, 2x AP multipliers, special tier cosmetics
- SOTU, SCOTUS, major hearings = pre-scheduled live events

**Annual:**
- Year-end Fingerprint (Spotify Wrapped for politics)
- Mythic class induction ceremony

---

## 15. META-GOAL

Users finish a session thinking: *I am slightly smarter and slightly angrier at the right things than I was 10 minutes ago.*

Not: *I wasted an hour.*

If meta-goal is satisfied, metrics follow.
