**Always:**
- Cite sources for every factual claim (link or screenshot)

## 5. GROWTH TACTICS

### Reply-to-grow (the real engine)
Target accounts 10K–500K followers. For each:
1. Notifications on
2. Reply within 90 seconds of post (first 5–10 replies get 10x visibility)
3. Add a fact, source, or sharp counterpoint. Never "This ^^" or "Based"
4. 50+ likes on our reply = original poster sometimes follows/mutes (either is signal)

Do 10–20x/day. This drives 60% of early growth.

### Quote-tweet strategy
When a political take goes mega-viral, quote within 2 hours with our data/take. Piggyback on tens of millions of impressions.

### Thread reposting
Best threads re-post 4 weeks later with small update. "Reposting because..." Evergreen.

### Don't do follow/unfollow
Shadow-suppressed, looks thirsty, kills brand.

### Micro-influencer collabs (later)
20 political Gen Z accounts 20K–100K. Offer free Legendary tier (skip to tier 10) for 1 X post + 1 TikTok at public launch.

### The meta-loop
Once platform ships, **our product becomes our growth engine.** Every battle clip is a micro-viral asset. Every Drop generates shareable takes.
